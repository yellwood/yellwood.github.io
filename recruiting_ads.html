Ph.D. Students

I am always looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students with strong research interests in security, machine learning, embedded system, computer architecture, and Internet of Things (IoT). If you are interested in researching any of these topic with me:

First, please apply for the Ph.D. program of ECE department at Northeastern.

Second, please mention my name as your preferred advisor, so your application materials can be easily found.

Note that you are very welcome to reach out via email, but please make sure that you have read at least one of my recent paper(s) before doing so, and let me know if any of them is of interest to you? And Why? in your emails.

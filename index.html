About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northeastern University. Previously I was with the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Chicago. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from UMass Amherst, and then I spent two years as a Post-Doc Fellow in the Florida Institute for Cybersecurity Research (FICS). I got my M.S. and B.E. degrees from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. In the past, I've also spent time at Broadcom Inc and Cryptography Research Inc.

Short Bio

I joined Northeastern in Fall 2020. Prior to that I was an assistant professor at UIC. I received my doctoral degree from UMass Amherst and worked as a Post-Doc Fellow in the Florida Institute for Cybersecurity Research. I got my M.S. and B.E. degrees from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.

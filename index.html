About

Since August 2020, I am an Assistant Professor in the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northeastern University. Previously, I was with the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Chicago. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from UMass Amherst in 2016, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Wayne Burleson. I also spent two years as a Post-Doc Fellow in the Florida Institute for Cybersecurity Research (FICS), where I worked with Prof. Mark Tehranipoor and Prof. Domenic Forte. I got my M.S. and B.E. degrees from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. In the past, I held internships at Broadcom Inc. and Cryptography Research Inc..
